Why you'll still see a couple of thumbnails: YouTube's embedded player no longer supports fully turning off related videos. With rel=0 (used here), the end-of-video and pause screens are restricted to showing videos from the same channel only — YouTube removed the option to disable this entirely back in 2018. This player also strips annotations, branding, and captions-by-default to keep the frame as quiet as possible. If you want zero suggestions of any kind, check "Loop this video" — it replays the same video instead of ending on a suggestion screen.
